# The GGB Philosophy
GGB (Grain, Green, Bean) isn't about restriction; it's about foundation. This week, we cook 3 times to cover 6 nights.

## The Shopping List (Combined)
* **Produce:** 3 onions, 2 leeks, 1 bag kale, 4 carrots, fresh ginger...
* **Pantry:** 3 cans Black Beans, 2 cans Kidney Beans, 1kg Quinoa...
* **The Shortcut:** If you're short on time, use pre-cooked jarred beans. If you're short on money, soak dry beans overnight.

## Prep
* **Keep it simple:** Use pre-cooked beans and vegetable broth from powder
* **Do it yourself:** See schedule to soak and cook beans in time and to prepare your own vegetable broth.
* **Clean it:** Wash all food and peal if necessary before using it. The recipes suppose you know that. Thoroughly rinse canned bean, since the fluid contain lots of salt and conservation agents. 

## The Schedule
* **Monday:** Cook **Chili Sin Carne** (Eat half, freeze half). Super easy, nothing to be handled in parallel.
* **Tuesday:** Cook **Green Dhal** (Eat half, freeze half). Easy one-pot with rice as side.
* **Wednesday:** Cook **Lemon Tahini Bean Bowl** (Eat half, fridge half).
* **Thursday:** Eat **Bean Bowl** Leftovers.
...and so on.
